About Barangay San Joaquin

Discover our community, meet our officials, and learn about our commitment to service

Our Community

Barangay San Joaquin is a vibrant and progressive community located in the municipality of Palo, Leyte, Philippines. Our barangay is committed to providing excellent public service and fostering a strong, united community through digital transformation and modern governance.

With a population of 2,844 residents across 688 households, we serve five puroks and are dedicated to making government services more accessible and efficient for all our constituents through our comprehensive e-Barangay platform.
